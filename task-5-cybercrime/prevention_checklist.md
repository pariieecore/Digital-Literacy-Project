#### **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:**

* ###### Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts
* ###### Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
* ###### Do not click on suspicious links in emails, messages, or social media
* ###### Always verify the authenticity of websites and emails before entering personal details
* ###### Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts
* ###### Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches





#### Financial / UPI Safety Tips:

* ###### Never share your OTP, PIN, or UPI credentials with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
* ###### Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them
* ###### Use only official banking and payment apps from trusted sources





#### **Reporting Cybercrime**

* ###### Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* ###### Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for immediate assistance
