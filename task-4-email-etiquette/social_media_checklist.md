### **Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students**

#### 

#### **DOs:**

* ###### Maintain a professional and respectful profile  
* ###### Think before posting and ensure content is appropriate 
* ###### Use privacy settings to control who can see your information  
* ###### Verify information before sharing to avoid misinformation 
* ###### Use social media for learning, networking, and opportunities



#### DON’Ts: 

* ###### Do not share personal or sensitive information (address, OTP, etc.) 
* ###### Avoid posting offensive or harmful content 
* ###### Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments 
* ###### Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages 
* ###### Do not use social media excessively during study time
